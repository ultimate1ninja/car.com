Car Showcase - BMW 3 Series E36

BMW 3 Series E36

Year: 1990-2000

Engine Options:

  • 1.6L I4 Petrol (102 hp)
  • 1.8L I4 Petrol (113 hp)
  • 1.8L I4 Petrol (138 hp) - Twin-cam version
  • 2.0L I6 Petrol (148 hp)
  • 2.5L I6 Petrol (189 hp)
  • 2.8L I6 Petrol (190 hp)
  • 1.7L I4 Diesel (89 hp)
  • 2.5L I6 Diesel (114 hp)

Transmission:

  • 4-speed automatic
  • 5-speed manual

Performance:

  • Top Speed: Up to 141 mph (for the 325i)
  • 0-60 mph: Approximately 6.4 seconds (325i model)

Body Styles:

  • Sedan (4-door)
  • Coupe (2-door)
  • Convertible (2-door)
  • Touring (5-door station wagon)
  • Compact (3-door hatchback)

Design and Features:

  • Balanced weight distribution, contributing to excellent handling.
  • Advanced features like airbags, ABS, and traction control.
  • Multi-link rear suspension for improved ride comfort and handling.

Notable Variants:

  • M3: High-performance version with up to 321 hp, widely respected in motorsports.

Legacy:

The E36 is praised for its driving dynamics, build quality, and design, remaining popular among car enthusiasts.
