Car Showcase - Volvo 240

Volvo 240

Year: 1974-1993

Engine Options:

  • 2.0L I4 Petrol
  • 2.1L I4 Petrol
  • 2.3L I4 Petrol
  • 2.4L I6 Diesel

Transmission:

  • 4-speed manual
  • 5-speed manual
  • 3-speed automatic
  • 4-speed automatic

Performance:

  • Top Speed: Approximately 115 mph (185 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph: Around 11 seconds

Dimensions:

  • Length: 4,820 mm (189.8 in)
  • Width: 1,710 mm (67.3 in)
  • Height: 1,420 mm (55.9 in)
  • Wheelbase: 2,640 mm (103.9 in)
  • Weight: 1,230-1,450 kg (2,712-3,197 lbs)

Design and Features:

  • Renowned for its boxy, durable design that emphasized safety and reliability.
  • The Volvo 240 was equipped with advanced safety features for its time, including reinforced doors, crumple zones, and seatbelt pretensioners.
  • A spacious interior with high-quality materials, providing comfort and practicality.
  • Available as a sedan and a station wagon, offering versatility for various needs.
  • Recognized for its long production run, with minimal design changes, reflecting its timeless appeal and robust engineering.

Notable Variants:

  • 240 GLT: A higher-performance variant with sportier features and more powerful engines.
  • 240 Turbo: Introduced in the early 1980s, this version featured a turbocharged engine, providing enhanced power and performance.
  • 240 DL: The base model, offering essential features with Volvo's legendary safety and durability.

Historical Significance:

The Volvo 240 is an iconic model, known for its exceptional safety standards, durability, and practicality. It became a symbol of reliability, particularly in the Northern European and North American markets, and played a crucial role in solidifying Volvo's reputation for building some of the safest cars on the road.

Legacy:

The Volvo 240 remains a beloved classic, admired by car enthusiasts for its build quality and timeless design. It has a loyal following, with many examples still on the road today, a testament to its longevity and the enduring appeal of its simple yet effective engineering.
